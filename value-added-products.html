Moringa

Botanical Name - Moringa oleifera

Moringa is one such multipurpose tree, commonly used in spices & cosmetic oils, and has various medicinal and therapeutic applications. Almost every part of the Moringa have four times calcium that of milk, seven times vitamin C that of oranges, three times potassium that of bananas, three times the spinach iron, four times the vitamin A in carrots, and two times the protein in milk.

Mango pulp :

Mango Pulp is prepared from selected varieties of Fresh Mango Fruit. Mango Pulp is perfectly suited for conversion to juices, nectars, drinks, jams, fruit cheese and various other kinds of beverages. It can also be used in puddings, bakery fillings, ice creams, yogurt, confectioneryand fruit meals for children and flavors for the food industry.

Guargum :

Botanical Name - Cyamopsis tetragonoloba

Guar gum is an agrochemical processed from endosperm of cluster bean. It is versed with multiple physical and chemical properties which make it a strong candidate in the world of excipients in food, cosmetic and pharmaceuticals. Also, it is largely used in paper, textile, explosive, oil well drilling and cosmetics industry. Industrial applications of guar gum are possible because of its ability to form hydrogen bonding with water molecule.
