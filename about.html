About Our Company

What makes us different?

As an independent firm, we “Exter International” are not bound by any corporate terms or conditions… Every quote, term of sale, currency, purchase and freight consignment is taylor-made to suit your needs. We always remain flexible and we always aim to offer competitive pricing.

We intend to leverage local resources and procurement expertise to offer the best sourcing solutions to buyers in the Agriculture sector. We will be pleased to receive inquiries for any product in any quantity, and we understand that often you will require consignments urgently.

Mutual cooperation, long term business relationship, effective consultation and unmatched quality consistency are the measures that are year by year increasing our client base from Europe, USA, UK, Canada, Middle East, South Asia and so on.

Mission

Promote and facilitate Indian agriculture to enhance its competitive advantage which creates a long-lasting advantage for all the stakeholders.

Vision

Become the frontrunner of the agriculture sector by providing unbeatable services and superior quality products at a desirable prices.

Value

We fully abide by ethics, integrity, reliability, superiority, the idea of community as a whole, and continuous improvements.
